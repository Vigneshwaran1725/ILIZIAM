About Us

The Department of Information Technology at Vel Tech Rangarajan Dr. Sagunthala R&D Institute of Science and Technology is proud to present a two-day symposium designed to inspire innovation and collaboration. This event serves as a dynamic platform for students and researchers to explore emerging technologies and diverse disciplines through a series of engaging technical and non-technical events. From paper presentations, and coding challenges to insightful panel discussions, and fun-filled cultural activities, the symposium promises a well-rounded experience for all participants. Join us as we push the boundaries of knowledge, creativity, and networking in an environment that fosters learning and excellence.

What is Our Goal?

The primary goal of this two-day symposium is to foster innovation, knowledge sharing, and interdisciplinary collaboration among students and researchers. Through a diverse range of technical and non-technical events, the symposium aims to:

  • Provide a platform for participants to showcase their technical expertise through coding challenges, paper presentations, and project demonstrations.
  • Encourage creative problem-solving and critical thinking by engaging in hackathons and case study competitions.
  • Facilitate networking opportunities with industry experts, academicians, and peers to bridge the gap between academia and real-world applications.
  • Promote teamwork, leadership, and communication skills through interactive workshops and non-technical events.
